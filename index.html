Dependencies

jQuery (1.something. Let me know if it does not work for you)

Installation

Copy jquery.caro.js to some appropriate place, link to it at your HTML, use $.caro($elem, opts);.

opts is an object that can contain delay (given in ms) and direction (either horizontal or vertical).

Take a look at the source of this page to figure out what kind of markup to use.
